# Elevation

Confetti has no blur-based elevation. Depth comes from two things: a small surface
ramp, and a hard offset shadow on hover.

## Surface ramp

Four steps. These are distinct surfaces, not opacity variants of one another —
the exact values came out of the homepage hero widget code.

## Lift

`--shadow-lift` is a 3px hard offset with no blur, paired with a
`translate(-2px, -2px)`. Buttons and interactive cards share it, which is what makes
the whole system read as one sticker language.

In dark mode the shadow inverts to cream — a dark shadow on a dark surface reads as
nothing, so the token repoints rather than the component changing.

<ShadowSpecimen />

Both the transform and the transition are tokens
(`--button-transform-hover`, `--button-transition-duration`), and both are dropped
under `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`.
