# Spacing

## Semantic roles

What components and layouts reference — never the raw scale. Two families sit over one
primitive ramp, so every primitive is adopted into a named role.

**Component scale** — the tight rhythm inside controls and cards (4 · 8 · 12 · 16 · 20 · 24):

<SpacingScale
  names={[
    'space-inset-xs',
    'space-inline',
    'space-inset-tight',
    'space-inset-sm',
    'space-stack',
    'space-inset-lg',
    'space-inset',
  ]}
/>

**Layout scale** — the larger page / section rhythm (32 · 40 · 48 · 64 · 80):

<SpacingScale
  names={[
    'space-stack-lg',
    'space-layout-sm',
    'space-layout',
    'space-layout-lg',
    'space-layout-xl',
  ]}
/>

## Primitive scale

The 4px-based ramp the semantic roles alias. Theme-independent — these do not
change with theme or mode.

## Radii

Semantic radii come first — `control` for buttons and pills, `container` for cards,
`chip` for small UI, `avatar` for dots. The primitive scale sits underneath.
