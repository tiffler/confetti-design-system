# Spacing

## Semantic roles

What component tokens actually reference. Components never reach past this layer
into the raw scale. Four steps — 4, 8, 16, 24 — listed smallest to largest.

## Primitive scale

The 4px-based ramp the semantic roles alias. Theme-independent — these do not
change with skin or mode.

## Radii

Semantic radii come first — `control` for buttons and pills, `container` for cards,
`chip` for small UI, `avatar` for dots. The primitive scale sits underneath.
