# Color

Every swatch below is read from `build/portfolio/tokens.json` for the skin and mode
selected in the toolbar — nothing on this page is hardcoded. Switch **Mode** in the
toolbar to see the semantic layer repoint while the token names stay put.

## Surfaces

The elevation ramp. `page` is the baseline, `raised` is canvas and widget surfaces,
`panel` is toolbar and sidebar chrome, `card` is the contrast interior.

## Action

Button-facing roles. Primary is the brand red, secondary the paper fill with an

<ColorGrid prefix="color-action-" />

## Accents

Four hues, each a tint/bold pair. `on-bold` and `on-subtle` are the foregrounds that
stay legible against each end of the pair — they diverge in dark mode, which is why
they are separate tokens.

<ColorGrid prefix="color-accent-" />

## Primitives

The raw ramp underneath. Primitives are theme-independent — they are emitted once
under `:root` and do not change with skin or mode. Semantic tokens are the only
layer that should be consumed by name.
